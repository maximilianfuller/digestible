Digestible

Make your list of articles easy for people to read.

With Digestible, people no longer have to revisit your site to finish the collection; they simply receive the content in staggered emails and read it at their leisure. And when they've read everything, the emails stop!

And all you have to do to create these automated emails is paste the links to your articles. We'll do the rest!

How it works

  • Create a subscription form.

    Here readers sign up to receive each article as an email.

  • Make the scheduled emails.

    Just paste the URL of each article and we'll generate the subject lines and content for you, or you can make the emails from scratch. Regardless, you can always edit them.

  • Link to your subscription form under your article collection.

    Now your readers have the option to casually read the articles with periodic emails.
